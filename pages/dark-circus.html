DARK CIRCUS

Genre:

Shoot 'em up

Engine:

Godot

Duration:

4 weeks

Team:

1

Platform:

PC / Console

Context

This project was developed in collaboration with Jonas Pelissou, who was in charge of music and sound design. The game is based on the Shoot 'em up genre, and our objective was to develop a complete, playable level.

Ambition

My ambition was to bring a layer of strategy to the classic shoot 'em up genre by introducing a clone mechanic. The player can place a clone that mimics their shooting, allowing them to attack from two separate positions simultaneously. Additionally, the player has a one-time ability to swap positions with the clone, enabling access to otherwise unreachable or locked areas, adding depth and tactical decision-making to the gameplay.

My Work

As a Game Designer, I was responsible for designing a new core gameplay feature: the clone mechanic, which adds a strategic layer to the traditional shoot 'em up experience.

As a Level Designer, I created the entire level with the goal of showcasing and making the most of this feature through thoughtful enemy placement and progression.

I also created the visual assets for the game, including the player, enemies, and the final boss, aiming for a cohesive and readable visual style that supports the gameplay.

As a Gameplay Programmer, I implemented all systems in a modular and clean way:

  • Player: movement, shooting, upgrades, clone placement and synchronized shooting, position swapping with the clone.
  • Enemies: movement patterns, shooting patterns, enemy spawning. boss behavior with multiple phases, phase synchronization with music
  • Boss: boss behavior with multiple phases, phase synchronization with music.
  • UI: life display, bomb counter, menus
